About Inavon Global

A Technology Partner for Tracking, Telematics & IoT Companies

More Than a Supplier — A Solution Enablement Partner

Inavon Global is a technology company focused on GPS tracking hardware, IoT sensors, telematics devices, gateways, accessories, and connected infrastructure. Our role is to support technology companies with reliable products, technical guidance, solution design, integration support, and scalable hardware supply.

Right Hardware for Every Use Case

We help companies select suitable devices and sensors based on project requirements, connectivity, accuracy, battery life, and platform compatibility.

Integration-Ready Approach

We help simplify the connection between hardware, sensors, gateways, and software platforms so solutions can be delivered faster.

Scalable Supply & Project Support

Whether you need devices for testing, pilot projects, or large-scale deployment, Inavon Global can support reliable supply and technical coordination.
